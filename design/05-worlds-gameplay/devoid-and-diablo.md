# Devoid and El Diablo's Lair

- **Devoid:** Hell dimension for **Devoider** fights; **unique world per clan or per player**; **resets after the fight** (instance).
- **El Diablo** is fought on the **Amplified nether roof** (RoofWitherListener: 500→600 HP, bedrock-breaking blue skulls, 25% mini-horde).
- **El Diablo’s Lair** (and **Devoid**, **Aquaria**, **Heaven’s Tower**, 6 elemental temples, starport, void/planets) can run on a **second Ryzen 7 9800X3D** machine with Lobby.
- **Storyline:** 6 Devoiders (36 withers) to reach El Diablo; El Diablo 3-phase ending in “Destroyer of Worlds” giant red wither that breaks bedrock; miniboss = giant netherite wither skeleton.
