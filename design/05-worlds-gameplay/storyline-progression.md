# Storyline progression

(See also [01-overview/storyline](01-overview/storyline.md).)

- **Leaving the Firmament:** Aether portal at y602 → **Aquaria** (underwater, glass cathedral) → **Neptune** miniboss → **Heavenly Tower** 3000 blocks (parkour, guards, spiral) → **Gabriel** quiz → **Rainbow Path** → **Lord’s Plateau**. **Golden Golem** checks El Diablo + Neptune; after first completion guards/Neptune skip.
- **Personality:** One **assigned at spawn**; after story, **quiz order** to **Ultimate** each; **Lord** reveals **Element + Zodiac** and **Elemental Temple** access.
- **Elemental:** One **temple per element**; quests, quizzes, minibosses, loot, enchants, advancements → **Guardian** battle; **Aether** last; all done = **Angel** → portal to **Galactic Journey**.
- **Post-game:** Lord’s Plateau Y5000, red carpet to Y-64 at 0,0; **Blessing** (double enchant); **Redeemer** on Easter Tuesday; **200%** achievements = **Main Theme** disc.
