# Seasons and hemisphere

- **Equator** (±1250 or ±500 blocks): **reduced/no weather**; **equator heat only**, no freezing; acts as **buffer** for new players.
- **North** = northern hemisphere seasons; **South** = southern; **Amplified** can be **southern only** so it’s distinct from Survival.
- **Christmas:** Chest sprites **Dec 24–Jan 1**; **snow week** at Christmas or in winter = more rain.
- **Hemisphere/season plugin:** Biome/position-based weather (north/south/equator) — doable, to be confirmed; **lobby music** by hemisphere was discussed.
- **Frost/Heatwave/Blizzard** are event/biome-based (see LWEvents); **no Frost/Heatwave in End/Nether**.
