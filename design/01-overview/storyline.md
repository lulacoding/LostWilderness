# Storyline

## 7-chapter structure and gates

The Lost Wilderness storyline is organised as **7 chapters**. Post-game content is the **Galactic Journey** (Angel → Arch Angel). Access to that progression is **locked behind**:

- Defeating **El Diablo**
- Beating the **Ender Dragon**
- Beating the **Father of Ender** (harder dragon on Amplified End)
- Unlocking **64 Ultimate personalities**
- Mastering the **5 elements**

So the “full” story is gated: players work through El Diablo, main bosses, personality system, and elemental content before reaching the Angel/Galactic Journey phase.

## El Diablo and Devoiders

The main antagonist is **El Diablo**, fought on the **Amplified nether roof**. The progression to him is:

- **6 withers per Devoider**
- **6 Devoiders** to reach El Diablo  
- So **36 withers total** in that arc.

El Diablo has a **3-phase fight**:

1. Devoider-level phase  
2. Buffed phase  
3. **“Destroyer of Worlds”** — giant red wither whose attacks can **break bedrock**

There is a **miniboss**: a **giant wither skeleton** in **netherite** (mentioned in the chats).

**RoofWitherListener** (plugin side): on **Survival** the roof boss is the **Devoider** (300 HP); on **Amplified** it is **El Diablo** (500→600 HP, bedrock-breaking blue skulls, 25% mini-horde).

## Devoid dimension

**Devoid** is a hell dimension used for **Devoider fights**. It is a **unique world per clan or per player** and **resets after the fight**. So Devoid is not a permanent overworld; it’s an instance for the boss encounter.

## Leaving the Firmament (post–El Diablo path)

After the main story, the path **“Leaving the Firmament”** is:

1. **Aether portal at y602**
2. **Aquaria** — underwater, glass cathedral
3. **Neptune miniboss**
4. **Heavenly Tower** — 3000 blocks total:
   - Parkour 0–1000
   - Guard at 1000
   - Parkour 1000–2000
   - Guard battle at 2000
   - Spiral 2000–3000
5. **Gabriel quiz**
6. **Rainbow Path**
7. **Lord’s Plateau**

A **Golden Golem** checks that the player has completed **El Diablo** and **Neptune**; after first completion, guards and Neptune can be skipped.

## Personality and elements

- **Personality:** One is **assigned at spawn**. After the story, players do a **quiz order** to reach **Ultimate** for each.
- **Lord** then reveals **Element** and **Zodiac** and grants access to **Elemental Temples**.
- **Elemental:** There is a **temple per element** with quests, quizzes, minibosses, loot, enchants, and advancements leading to a **Guardian battle**. **Aether** is last; when all are done the player becomes **Angel** and gets the **portal to Galactic Journey**.

## Galactic Journey (Starport)

**Galactic Journey** uses a **Starport** and **Multiverse** worlds. The order discussed is:

**Venus → Mercury → Moon → Mars → Jupiter → Saturn → Uranus → Neptune → Pluto**

Each has **lore**, a **boss**, **custom items**, and **Planet Enchants**. This is the post-game “Angel” content.

## Post-game rewards

- **Lord’s Plateau** at **Y5000**, with a **red carpet to Y-64 at 0,0**.
- **Blessing** (double enchant).
- **Redeemer** encounter on **Easter Tuesday**.
- **200% achievements** (Vanilla + custom) reward: **Main Theme disc**.
