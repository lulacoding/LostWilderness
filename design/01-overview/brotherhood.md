# Brotherhood and Epochians

## Brotherhood

The **Brotherhood** is a **secret group** in the Lost Wilderness world. From the chats:

- They have a **temple** and **Bible library** in **Amplified**, in a **desert/mesa** area described as “middle east” in feel.
- The world is kept **vague** so that “**truth seekers**” can find it rather than it being obvious.
- The Brotherhood is **“writing a server world bible”** — in-world lore and “Sam verses” (e.g. crops in Spring, Nether disease cures, Amplified portal, etc.). So the in-game “Bible” is both flavour and a way to hint at mechanics and story.
- **Bible in MC:** KJV (King James Version) is referenced; chiselled bookshelves and a “library of Rome” are part of the concept.

## Epochians

**Epochians** are players who were on the server at **Day 0** (the “Epoch” — the first day of the calendar, with the subtitle “A new era begins”). From the chats:

- **“Epochians will be invited to be higher up degrees for The Brotherhood.”**
- So Day 0 players get a special role in the secret society.
- The Brotherhood **“won’t really do much for the first however long until it gets bigger”**; members will be across different teams. One example given: “my team will be like Eureka Republic with this banner.”

## Lore and factions

- **Pillagers** are described as following **the Wither** (antichrist figure in the server’s mythos).
- **Totems** have lore attached.
- The server’s “Bible” and Brotherhood content are part of the long-term RP and discovery, not front-and-center in the first weeks.
