# Biome and event music

- **Frost:** Coldplay Clocks / Dark Cave style; flash-freeze .wav or powder snow; plays in frost radius, fades when leaving.
- **Blizzard:** Sinnoh Route 216 day/night; Blizzard Eclipse = eclipse theme.
- **Heatwave:** PMD Quicksand Pit; oriental metal / Egypt boss.
- **Ambush:** 5–20 dungeon mobs; “Oh No! Ambushed!”; PMD Monster House style.
- **Diablo:** Hoenn Legendary orchestral, bells + choir + drums.
- **Music logic:** Event/boss/war/biome/Y/dimension/Normal or Amplified; **intro + credits**; **Xmas 1–2** by hemisphere.
